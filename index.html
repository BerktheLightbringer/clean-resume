About Me

PMP - Senior Site Manager

As a Senior Site Manager at Intec Energy Solutions, I oversee and direct large-scale solar projects in various countries, such as Mozambique, France, Poland, Denmark, Cameroon, and Equatorial Guinea. I have successfully delivered multiple solar power plants with a total capacity of over 500 MWp, ensuring high-quality standards, timely completion, and customer satisfaction. I am currently pursuing a master's degree in Engineering Management at Middle East Technical University, where I am enhancing my skills and knowledge in project planning, risk management, quality control, and leadership. I am also the founder of The Light Bringer, Turkey's first peer-to-peer solar leasing platform, which aims to provide affordable and sustainable energy solutions to communities and businesses. I have a passion for renewable energy and a talent for problem-solving, communication, and client relationships. I continuously stay updated on the latest developments and innovations in the solar industry, and I strive to deliver the most efficient and effective solutions that meet the needs and challenges of the customers. My goal is to contribute to the global transition to clean and renewable energy sources, and to make a positive impact on the environment and society.

I’m passionate about technology and developing creative solutions to whichever problem I come across. I’m an avid reader and music lover.
